# What outcomes teams aim for (8-10 minutes)

The main outcome of an AI hackathon is usually a **working prototype** that proves an idea, not a finished enterprise platform.

## Common hackathon outcomes
- a starter agent that can answer or route a specific type of request
- a workflow that uses GCP services to retrieve, classify, or summarize information
- a demo that shows how the idea could save time, improve service, or reduce manual effort
- a clearer view of what should be built next after the hackathon

## What a good outcome looks like
- the prototype runs
- the team can explain the user problem clearly
- the solution is grounded in a real business need
- the demo shows value, even if the implementation is still basic

## What a weak outcome looks like
- the team built something interesting but cannot explain why it matters
- the team talks only about technology and not the user problem
- the prototype depends on guesses instead of real business context
