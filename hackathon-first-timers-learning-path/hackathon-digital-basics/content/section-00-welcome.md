# Welcome: What this course will set up for you (3 minutes)

This is **Course 1 of 4** in the **First-Time Hackathon Pathway**. Start here before taking any of the later courses.

This course is for learners who think:

- "I do not know what an AI hackathon actually is"
- "I am not sure what we are supposed to produce in two days"
- "I come from a business team, so I do not know what my role will be"
- "I want the big picture before I touch the technical steps"

By the end of this course you should be able to:

- explain what an AI hackathon is
- describe the outcomes teams usually aim for
- explain why organizations run hackathons
- recognize how business-focused departments help a team succeed
- know what to expect across the two-day agenda
