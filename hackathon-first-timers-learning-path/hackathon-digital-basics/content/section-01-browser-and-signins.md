# What an AI hackathon is (8-10 minutes)

An AI hackathon is a short, time-boxed event where small teams use AI tools and cloud services to build or improve a practical solution.

In this pathway, the solution work is done on **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)** using guided starter materials.

## What makes it a hackathon
- the time is limited
- the problem is real enough to matter
- teams move quickly from idea to working prototype
- learning and experimentation are part of the goal

## What an AI hackathon is not
- not a college exam
- not a full production launch
- not a requirement to become a software engineer in two days
- not a solo activity where only technical people contribute

## In practical terms, your team will usually:
1. understand the challenge
2. review the starter guidance
3. set up the provided GCP-based environment
4. adapt the starter agent or workflow
5. test the result
6. explain what you built and why it matters
