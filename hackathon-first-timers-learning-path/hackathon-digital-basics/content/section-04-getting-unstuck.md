# The role of business-focused departments (10-12 minutes)

Business-focused teams are not "extra people in the room." They are often the reason the team builds something useful instead of something merely impressive.

## What business participants contribute
- real process knowledge
- customer context
- policy and operational constraints
- success criteria
- a clearer view of what would make the prototype adoptable after the hackathon

## Example departments and what they add
- **Operations**: where work slows down, where handoffs fail, and what efficiency matters
- **Customer support or complaints**: the real language customers use and what a useful response should include
- **Finance**: where risk, approvals, and measurable value show up
- **Product or business analysis**: what problem is worth solving first and how success should be measured

## Why this matters in an AI hackathon
Technical teams may know how to build an agent. Business teams know whether the agent is solving the right problem.

The best hackathon teams combine both.
