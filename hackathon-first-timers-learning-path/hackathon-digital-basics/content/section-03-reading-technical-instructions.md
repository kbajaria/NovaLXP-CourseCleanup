# Why organizations run AI hackathons (10 minutes)

Organizations do not run hackathons just to fill two days on the calendar. They run them to learn faster and reduce the gap between ideas and tested prototypes.

## Why companies sponsor these events
- to explore where AI can help a real business process
- to build momentum across technical and non-technical teams
- to test ideas before committing to a larger delivery program
- to teach teams how modern platforms such as GCP support rapid experimentation

## Why this matters to learners
When you know the reason for the event, you make better choices. You stop asking only "Did we build something?" and start asking:

- Did we solve the right problem?
- Did we prove value clearly?
- Did we learn enough to decide what should happen next?

## The real value is often broader than the demo
- shared understanding across teams
- clearer requirements
- early evidence for investment decisions
- better confidence using AI tooling in a safe, guided setting
