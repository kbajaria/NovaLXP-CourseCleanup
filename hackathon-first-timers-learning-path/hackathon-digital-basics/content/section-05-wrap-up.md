# What to expect over two days and wrap-up (5-7 minutes)

Across two days, you should expect a flow like this:

## Day 1
- understand the challenge and starter materials
- access the provided GCP environment
- get the starter solution running
- make the first guided changes

## Day 2
- extend the solution
- test and refine it
- connect the work back to the business problem
- prepare a short explanation or demo

## A good mindset for the event
- you are here to learn quickly, not look perfect
- the team should keep tying technical steps back to the business need
- a smaller working prototype is better than an ambitious unfinished idea

## You should now be able to:
- explain what an AI hackathon is
- describe the outcomes teams usually aim for
- explain why organizations run them
- describe how business-focused departments contribute
- picture how the two-day flow will feel
