## What is a roadmap?
A roadmap is a communication tool that shows direction and priorities over time so teams and stakeholders can align.

Roadmaps are great for:
- clarifying what matters most next
- setting expectations about sequencing and timing
- aligning work to goals and initiatives

## What a roadmap is not
A roadmap is not a sprint plan or a task list. It usually avoids low-level execution detail.

## Common roadmap formats
- Timeline roadmap (planned work over time)
- Now / Next / Later (useful when dates are uncertain)
- Release roadmap (grouped by release)
- Portfolio roadmap (multiple products or teams)

## Good roadmap habits
- Write items as outcomes when possible ("Reduce onboarding time"), not only outputs ("Build onboarding form v2").
- Tailor what you show to the audience.
- Keep it current.
