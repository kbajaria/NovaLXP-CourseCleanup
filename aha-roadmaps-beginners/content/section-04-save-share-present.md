# Save, share, and present your roadmap (8–10 minutes)

Goal: Save a reusable view and choose the right sharing method for your audience.

## Step 1 — Save a view (3 minutes)
Saved views help you return to the same filtered/customized roadmap and share it consistently.

### Visual references (Aha! public knowledge base)
- **Saved views (overview)**
https://support.aha.io/aha-roadmaps/support-articles/collaboration/saved-views

- **Create saved views**
https://support.aha.io/aha-roadmaps/support-articles/collaboration/create-saved-views~7444634987519214282

## Step 2 — Share internally (2 minutes)
Most teams share by sending the saved view URL to teammates.

### Visual reference (Aha! public knowledge base)
**How to share a report, roadmap, or view**
https://support.aha.io/aha-roadmaps/getting-started/how-to/share-report-roadmap-view

## Step 3 — Present to stakeholders (3–4 minutes)
If you need a “deck-like” experience, use Aha! presentations and add saved views to slides.

### Visual references (Aha! public knowledge base)
- **Introduction to presentations**
https://support.aha.io/aha-roadmaps/support-articles/presentations/aha-presentations-introduction

- **Publish a presentation (webpage or PDF)**
https://support.aha.io/aha-roadmaps/support-articles/presentations/publish-aha-presentations~7444650402677416636

## Step 4 — Share externally (optional, 2 minutes)
If you need to share outside Aha!, you can share saved views as webpages (with security options).
